# Tanqory Design Ethos

Efficiency, intuition, and style combine to empower merchants with **data-rich views**, **action-driven interfaces**, and **dynamic interactions**.  
These principles define the Tanqory design ethos and guide every product decision.

## What "Design Ethos" Means
Tanqory's design ethos focuses on **clarity and speed** for the daily tasks merchants perform.  
Interfaces use space efficiently so merchants can see more data at once, navigate with familiar icons, and act quickly—without unnecessary words or clutter.

## Assign Clear Meaning
Visual language must communicate at a glance.

- **Color with purpose** – Red warns, green confirms, yellow signals caution.  
- **Universal icons** – Import, export, edit, and print use well-known symbols for instant recognition.  
- **Hierarchy by design** – Size, weight, and color show importance and guide scanning.

## Increase Density, Maintain Usability
Use space thoughtfully to match the task:

- **High density** for data-heavy tables or dashboards.  
- **Lower density** for focused tasks like detailed forms.  
- Group related information with spacing and subtle color shifts to help quick decision-making.

## Craft Engaging Interactions
Interfaces should feel **responsive and alive**.

- Smooth hover transitions and clear pressed states give physical feedback.  
- Subtle animations (like a checkbox tick) create familiarity and confidence.  
- All primary actions provide immediate, predictable response.

## Stay Predictable
Consistency reduces cognitive load.

- Elements that look alike behave alike across web and mobile.  
- Reuse patterns and signifiers so merchants can apply learned behaviors everywhere.

## Practical Checklist
- [ ] Color roles documented and applied consistently.  
- [ ] Icons follow a shared library and naming convention.  
- [ ] Interactive states provide instant, accessible feedback.  
- [ ] Density adapts to context while preserving readability.

By following this **Design Ethos**, every Tanqory experience remains **fast, intuitive, and unmistakably Tanqory**.
