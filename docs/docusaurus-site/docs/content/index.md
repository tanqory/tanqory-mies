# Content Guidelines

Clear, consistent content creates better user experiences. These guidelines ensure your writing aligns with Tanqory Mies principles.

## Content Philosophy

Our content follows the same "less is more" philosophy as our design system:
- **Concise and purposeful** - Every word serves a function
- **Human-centered** - Written for real people, not systems
- **Globally accessible** - Clear across cultures and languages
- **Systematically consistent** - Unified voice and style

## Getting Started

1. **Start with basics** - Review writing fundamentals
2. **Learn the style** - Understand our tone and voice
3. **Practice inclusivity** - Write for diverse audiences
4. **Test globally** - Consider localization needs

Good content makes complex things simple and helps users accomplish their goals efficiently.
